America

The Americas, sometimes collectively called America, are a landmass comprising the totality of North and South America.The Americas make up most of the land in Earth's Western Hemisphere and comprise the New World.

Africa

Africa is the world's second-largest and second-most populous continent after Asia. At about 30.3 million km2 (11.7 million square miles) including adjacent islands, it covers 20% of Earth's land area and 6% of its total surface area

Asia

Asia is the largest continent in the world by both land area and population. It covers an area of more than 44 million square kilometers,about 30% of Earth's total land area and 8% of Earth's total surface area. The continent, which has long been home to the majority of the human population,was the site of many of the first civilizations. Its 4.7 billion people[13] constitute roughly 60% of the world's population
